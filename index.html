ABOUT

Welcome to the NewYearTrees NFTs - a collection of 1000 all unique 3D Christmas Trees stored on the Solana blockchain. Each NFT is generated randomly from over 70 different traits.

It was created a long time ago when lonely Lumberjack decided to make his own collection of Christmas trees.

We offer a Fair Launch Protocol (FLP) launch so every NFT is distributed fairly and randomly according to the high standards of the NFT distribution system.

Most loyal project investors will have the ability to decide future project directions. It will be either a huge gift for a part of the project's overall total income and royalties from all the marketplaces or the same amount of money will be given to charity.

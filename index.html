ABOUT

Welcome to the NewYearTrees NFTs - a collection of 1000 all unique 3D Christmas Trees stored on the Solana blockchain. Each NFT is generated randomly from over 70 different traits.

It was created a long time ago when lonely Lumberjack decided to make his own collection of Christmas trees.

We offer a Fair Launch Protocol (FLP) launch so every NFT is distributed fairly and randomly according to the high standards of the NFT distribution system.

Most loyal project investors will have the ability to decide future project directions. It will be either a huge gift for a part of the project's overall total income and royalties from all the marketplaces or the same amount of money will be given to charity.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

01 - What are NewYearTrees NFTs?
+
NewYearTrees NFTs are a collection of 1000 all unique Christmas Trees stored on the Solana blockchain. Each NFT is generated randomly from over 70 different traits.
02 - Why do I need NewYearTrees NFT?
+
Besides allowing you to show off your NFTs to one and all, it’s also a great New Year or Christmas gift to your friends. All holders of NewYearTrees NFTs own the full rights to their NFTs, and long-term holders will benefit from future rewards and additional NFT drops.
03 - How are NewYearTrees NFT distributed?
+
In general 1000 + 77 NFTs were created.

→ 100 are reserved and allocated for presale.
→ 900 will be available in the main sale for the mint price.
→ 77 most legendary NFTs are reserved for future distribution for most active and loyal holders. They can’t be minted or purchased in any way.
04 - What is the minting price?
+
As we offer a Fair Launch Protocol (FLP) launch each NewYearTrees NFT will cost from 0.5 SOL to 1 SOL + GAS (additional transaction fee which is about 0.01 SOL) to mint.
05 - Mint date
+
NewYearTrees NFTs can be minted from our website on 25 December from 9 pm UTC onwards until all are distributed.
06 - How many NewYearTrees NFTs can I buy?
+
Unlimited NewYearTrees NFTs can be minted per address but only one per transaction.
07 - Presale
+
Our team has a strong will to expand our community hence we decided to make a 100 NewYearTrees NFT presale. We will officially announce the date few days before presale. This will cost you about 0.5 SOL, unlike main mint where mint price will be higher.
08 - Rarity
+
There are different types of NewYearTrees with over 70 different attributes, such as Christmas Tree Color, Backgrounds, Presents, Decorations, Lights, and others.

Some attributes are rarer than others. Minting is fully random, and we can’t anyhow control that process. A full rarity chart will be released once the public mint has ended.
09 - Which marketplaces will NewYearTrees NFTs be on?
+
We have already applied and are currently waiting for the response from Solana Art. More marketplaces will follow soon after.
